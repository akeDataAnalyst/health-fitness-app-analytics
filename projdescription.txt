About the role
Ruby Labs is hiring a Junior Data Analyst to join our fast-paced and rapidly growing team. In this role, you will be working to create new reports and improve current reports, and working to ensure that we have safe, correct data to make decisions.

Work with a variety of products and clients, building their future data environment, and play a critical role in leveraging data to drive informed decision-making

Key Responsibilities
Partner with Product Owners and Data Teams.

Control and monitor Marketing and Product metrics in Mixpanel and Tableau.

Create new reports and collaborate with product owners and developers to maintain high-quality event tracking by aligning on event naming and mapping standards for new features and A/B tests.

Identify trends, patterns, and anomalies in data to support business insights.

Use SQL to get and analyse data for reports and team requests.

Collaborate closely with the data team to ensure consistency in reporting and data quality across the organisation.
